About the Developer

SIT ASSAN BETA app is developed by a student of Silicon Institute of Technology.

The developer was inspired to develop the app after experiencing the challenges of navigating multiple platforms and websites to access his college's services. He realized that there was a need for a more centralized and user-friendly platform that would allow students to access all of their college services in one place.

He wanted to create a more convenient and user-friendly way for students to access their college's ERP system, generate barcodes, and get online study materials. He also wanted to create an app that would help students stay organized and on top of their academics.

He is passionate about technology and is committed to creating innovative solutions that can make people's lives easier.

Here are some of the highlighted reasons why the developer made SIT ASSAN BETA app:

  • To make it easier for students to access their college's ERP system and online study materials.
  • To help students stay organized and on top of their academics.
  • To create a more convenient and user-friendly way for students to access their college's services.
  • To make a difference in the lives of students and to help them succeed in their studies.
